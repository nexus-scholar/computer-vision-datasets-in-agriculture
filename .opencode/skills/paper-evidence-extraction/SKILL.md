## Extract

- stable identity and document type;
- dataset introduced/used/mentioned;
- crop, task, modality, sensors, scale, labels, geography, time, splits, license/access;
- model, baselines, training/evaluation protocol, metrics;
- robustness, missing input, corruption, cross-domain, calibration, uncertainty, foundation-model use;
- limitations stated by authors and limitations inferred from methods;
- exact page/section/table/figure for each important statement;
- relevance to the project thesis.

Separate author claims, observed facts, and your inference. Never invent missing fields.
