## Include when

The paper introduces, benchmarks, extends, or experimentally uses an agricultural computer-vision dataset, or provides a directly relevant method for segmentation, multimodal fusion, robustness, domain shift, calibration, uncertainty, phenotyping, robotics, remote sensing, hyperspectral, or 3D vision.

## Exclude when

The citation is incidental, the work is outside agriculture/vision, no relevant experiment exists, identity is unresolved, full text is unavailable for a claim requiring it, or the record is a duplicate.

## Required fields

Decision, reason, screening stage, actual dataset use, datasets, task, modality, evidence location, reviewer, date, and uncertainty.
