description: Maintain auditable research claims linked to primary evidence, contradictions, uncertainty, and required experiments.
compatibility: opencode
metadata:
  project: agri-cv
  workflow: writing
---

Every claim needs: claim ID, exact statement, scope, evidence IDs, source locations, contradictory evidence, strength, uncertainty, status, reviewer, date, and required validation.

Statuses: proposed, supported, contested, rejected, superseded.

Do not upgrade a claim to supported from a model summary alone. Primary paper evidence or reproducible project results are required.
